Chocolate Cake

Description

This chocolate crockpot cake is incredibly moist and delicious, and it's so easy to make in your slow cooker. Serve it warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.

Make sure to follow all the steps in great detail to get the most amazing chocolate cake ever

Ingredients

  • cooking spray
  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • ¾ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Steps

  1. Spray the crock of a large slow cooker with cooking spray.
  2. Whisk sugar, flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, baking powder, and salt together in a large bowl.
  3. Whisk milk, oil, eggs, and vanilla together in a medium bowl until well combined. Slowly whisk in boiling water.
  4. Add wet ingredients to dry ingredients and mix until well combined; pour batter into the prepared slow cooker.
  5. Cover and cook on Low until cake has no wet spots on top and is pulling away from the sides of the crock, about 3 hours.
  6. Turn off slow cooker and let rest for 30 minutes before serving.
