Brownie recipe

Description

I recently found myself in need of a good brownie recipe. My love for brownies began when I was in school I tried so many brownies from different shops and I tried following a few recipes myself. But not one recipe satisfied me. So I decided to make my own resipe and I finally hit that taste with one secret ingredient: Symphony bars. I used the toffee-almond bars and I think that was the right idea. But, if you are baking for a picky eater or someone with allergies, the smooth chocolate makes amazing brownies too.

Make sure to follow all the steps in great detail to get the most amazing brownies ever

Ingredients

  • 1 (18-ounce) box brownie mix (such as Ghirardelli)
  • ¼ cup water
  • ⅓ cup vegetable oil
  • 1 egg
  • 1-2 (7.37-ounce) Giant Symphony bars or 2-3 (4.25-ounce) XL Symphony bars, broken into squares

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F and grease an 8x8-inch pan.
  2. Combine the mix, water, oil, and egg in a bowl and stir until fully incorporated.
  3. Pour half of the batter into the pan, then arrange the Symphony squares in an even layer across the batter. Pour the remaining batter into the pan.
  4. Bake the brownies for 40-45 minutes.
