White Sauce for Pasta

Description

I have recently fallen in love with white sauce pasta, this is the simplest recipe ever and its kind of hard to mess up this kind of sauce

Make sure to follow all the steps in great detail to get the most amazing white sauce ever for your delicious pasta or spaghetti

Ingerdients

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons margarine
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 Â½ cups boiling water
  • 1 cup 2% milk
  • ground white pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Melt butter and margarine in a saucepan over medium-low heat.
  3. Add flour; stir until well blended, 1 to 2 minutes.
  4. Continue to cook and stir until thickened and lightly browned, about 5 minutes.
  5. Increase the heat to medium and whisk in water until smooth.
  6. Stir in milk; cook and stir until hot and thickened, 2 to 3 more minutes.
  7. Season with white pepper.

And there you have your delicious white sauce ready to use
